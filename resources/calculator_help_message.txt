Calculator Mode Help Instructions
You may interact with the software using the following commands

1. help: Show help information
2. exit: Exit the program
3. clear: Clear the console
4. back: Go back to the main menu

5. revenue: Show the total revenue so far
6. value: Show the total value of the inventory in its current state
7. cost: Show the total cost of the business
8. profit: Show the total profit of the business
